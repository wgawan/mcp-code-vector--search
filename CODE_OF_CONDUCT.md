# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone. By participating, you agree to uphold this code.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate.
- Give and accept constructive feedback graciously.
- Assume good intent; seek to understand before responding.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or demeaning comments.
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without explicit permission.
- Disruptive behavior that derails productive collaboration.

## Reporting
If you observe or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers through a private channel (security advisory, direct message, or email once published). Avoid posting sensitive reports in public issues.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community spaces. Decisions will be made in good faith to maintain a safe and productive environment.
